Semantic Vega Editor v1.31

- Lean SSVG export: removed embedded CompactVEM from SSVG metadata.
- Clarified architecture: SSVG DOM attributes are the source of semantic truth; P3-VEM should generate CompactVEM after loading the SSVG.
- Replaced nativeCompactVemEmbedded with compactVemEmbedded: false and added recommendedConsumerAction for downstream consumers.
- Dropped diagnostic-heavy cohort summaries from SSVG metadata, including styleSummary, geometrySummary, textSummary, and dataSummary, because these are duplicative and should be regenerated by P3-VEM from the live SVG DOM.
- Kept lightweight metadata needed for downstream manipulation: cohort/container ids, author labels, viz parts, data roles, selectors, representative element ids, render status, and visibility mode.
- Renamed embedded readiness metadata to ssvgReadiness to reflect that it validates SSVG attributes/selectors rather than validating a CompactVEM snapshot.
