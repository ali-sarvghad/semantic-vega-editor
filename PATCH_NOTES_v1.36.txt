Semantic Vega Editor v1.36

- Fixed portable rendering of continuous legend gradients in exported SSVG.
- Normalizes gradient paint references such as url(blob:...#gradient_0) to local url(#gradient_0) references during cohort discovery and SSVG export.
- Preserves local SVG <defs><linearGradient> definitions as the authoritative gradient source.
- Adds continuous legend ramps as first-class renderable cohorts with type `legend-gradient` and p3-viz-part `legend-color-ramp`.
- Adds legend-gradient cohorts to legend container child metadata, so downstream tools such as P3-VEM can target, explain, and manipulate color ramps.
- Keeps the lean SSVG architecture: P3-VEM should still generate CompactVEM from the live SSVG DOM after import.
